
PROJECT TITLE:
"One Path"

GITHUB:
https://github.com/luvevelina/MA1805-FINAL-.git 

DESCRIPTION OF GAME:
this game is based on an Agatha Christie quote. the game works as an ordinary maze but you cant go back (left button). 
the player in the game resembles you, how you go through life. the conception stage is the easiest as theres only one path. 
the birth level teaches you theres more than 1 path. in the life level, you get to 
explore and create your own path. i added multiple exits for this level to show that there is no
"right path". signifying that life has no true end goal, but you as a person choose your own goal.
in the death stage your character follows an ecg line. one path. one ending. showing that the end result
is always the same.
at the end your control gets revoked. the player moves by itself and wont stop nor listen to your controls.
the end signifys reflection of your journey.
the game ends when your player goes off the screen not to be seen again.

TECHNICAL APPROACH:
i used rectangles and lines to create the mazes
i focused most of my coding on how the player can react with the game
i struggled with implementing some of my main ideas:
- adding a gradient in DEATH where as the player moves along the x axis the screen becomes white
signifying death
- i wanted to add a timer that players could reflect on at the ending
- i was unable to add barriers to the walls for level 4 as it was near impossible to complete the level with it


if i were to develop my game further id:
- add imagery to the background and certain sounds that resinate with the level
- introduce different stages within life

SOURCES OF INSPIRATION
my main source of inspiration came from the agatha christie quote "I learned...  that one can never go back,
 that one should not ever try to go back — that the essence of life is going forward. 
 Life is really a one way street, isn't it?”
